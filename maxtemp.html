Latitude vs Temperature

Across the 546 cities collected, the data set showed an easily comprehensible pattern with the rise and fall of temperatures depending on latitude. The scatter plot provides a general trend of what was discovered. The plot illustrates that the closer you are to the equator, the higher the temperatures rises (envisioned as the red plot points), whereas the further you are, the cooler the temperatures will be (shown as the blue plot points).
