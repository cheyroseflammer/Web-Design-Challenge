Latitude vs Wind Speed

From the 546 cities obtained for analysis, there doesn't seem to get any general relationship between latitude and wind speed. It seems a majority of areas that experinced high wind speeds would be at the latitude points of -20 (or 20 degress south of equator) and at the latitude point 40 (or 40 degrees north of the equator).
